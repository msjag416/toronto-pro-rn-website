Recovery Questions

Common Questions About Private Recovery Nursing

Do I need a nurse after a tummy tuck or 360 lipo?

Most surgeons strongly recommend that you're not alone for the first 24 hours after a tummy tuck, 360 liposuction, BBL, or any procedure with general anesthesia. The first night is when complications like hematoma, breathing problems, nausea, and fainting are most likely. A private recovery nurse monitors your vital signs, manages your drains and pain medication, and responds immediately if something changes while your surgeon's office is closed.

Can a nurse stay overnight with me after plastic surgery in Toronto?

Yes. We provide continuous overnight nursing at your home, hotel, or recovery suite anywhere in the GTA. Your nurse stays awake through the night monitoring vital signs, tracking drain output, timing medications, managing nausea, and helping you safely get up for the first time.

Who takes care of my drains after a tummy tuck?

Your recovery nurse handles all drain care: stripping the tubing, emptying and measuring output, recording it for your surgeon, and watching for signs of blockage or infection. Most patients find drains the most stressful part of recovery, and having an experienced nurse manage them removes the guesswork.

Can I recover in a hotel after plastic surgery in Toronto?

Yes. Hotel recovery is common for out-of-town patients who travel to Toronto for surgery. We provide discreet private nursing in your hotel room, including overnight monitoring before you're cleared to fly home, and we coordinate directly with your surgeon's post-op instructions.

How much does a private nurse cost after cosmetic surgery in Toronto?

Cost depends on the procedure and the length of care. We offer flat-rate packages for the first 10 hours or first 24 hours after surgery, with transparent pricing provided at your consultation before you book. There are no hidden fees and no markups on supplies.

Is a private recovery nurse covered by OHIP?

No, OHIP doesn't cover private duty or cosmetic recovery nursing. Cosmetic recovery care is paid privately. For injury files, we also accept engagements funded through case management firms, law firms, and auto insurance medical-rehabilitation benefits, invoiced directly to the funding party.
